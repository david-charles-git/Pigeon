What is PIGEON

PIGEON is the one-stop-shop for businesses to connect with their customers and transact via video.

With PIGEON, you can:

- create a tailored, shareable business profile
- take bookings and integrate with your calendar
- host 1-2-1 video sessions
- Set your own hourly rate up to £100
- get paid for your time

automatically, by the minute
- reconcile to your accounts

all via one app.

PIGEON takes the hassle out of the admin for online video transactions so you have the time to focus on what you do best.

Our unique technology calculates and collects payment directly from the duration of the video session. It

charges your customer automatically and immediately - and you get paid within 3 days.
